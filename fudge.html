Fudge

What's so special about our fudge?

Other than the fact that it's made fresh in house, with only real butter and cream and that there are over 300 flavours...

THE TASTE!!!

There's a good chance that our fudge will be one of the best tasting things you ever put in your mouth. And quite possibly the best $5.00 you'll ever spend ;)

With somewhere around 30 flavours available daily, get ready for some tough decisions.
